FAQs for Our Agri-Marketplace

1. What is the purpose of this agri-marketplace?

Our agri-marketplace connects farmers and buyers to facilitate the sale of agricultural products directly. We aim to support local farmers while providing buyers with fresh produce.

2. How do I create an account?

3. What types of products can I find on the marketplace?

You can find a variety of agricultural products, including fruits, vegetables, grains. Each vendor may have different offerings, so browse through the listings for availability.

4. How do I contact a seller?

Each product listing includes contact information for the seller. You can reach out directly via phone or email to discuss details or place an order.

5. Do you handle payment processing?

No, we do not handle payment processing. Payments are to be arranged directly between buyers and sellers. Please discuss payment options with the seller when placing an order.

6. How is delivery managed?

Delivery is not handled by our platform. Buyers and sellers are responsible for arranging the delivery of products. We recommend discussing delivery options and logistics directly with the seller.

7. What should I do if I encounter an issue with a seller?

If you experience any issues with a seller, we recommend reaching out to them directly first. If you need further assistance, you can contact our support team, and we will do our best to help.

8. Can I list my products for sale?

Yes! If you are a farmer or seller, you can create a listing for your products. Please log in to your account and navigate to the "Shop" section to get started.

9. Is there a fee to use the marketplace?

There are no fees for buyers. Sellers may be subject to listing fees or commissions based on their sales, so please check our terms and conditions for more details.

10. How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can submit your comments or suggestions through the "Contact Us" section on our website.
